About Me

Hi, my name is Galardo and I am currently 19 years old. I am the first of two children born in Kediri, East Java on October 22, 2005. But in 2010 my parents and I moved to Bali. I spent my childhood there, playing, and learning from elementary school to high school. I learned a lot of things while I was in Bali, both academic things and life lessons that were very valuable to me.

August 19, 2023, I decided to continue my studies at Bina Nusantara University. I took a double degree study program, namely Digital Business Innovation. But because this campus is located in Malang, East Java, I had to move to Malang and made me meet new friends. This is where I am now, studying, playing, doing something fun, and spending time.
